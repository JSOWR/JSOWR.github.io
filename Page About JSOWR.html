About JSOWR

The Jain Society of the Waterloo Region (JSOWR) is a vibrant and dedicated community committed to preserving and promoting the rich spiritual and cultural heritage of Jainism. Our mission is to foster a deeper understanding of Jain principles such as non-violence (ahimsa), truthfulness (satya), and compassion (karuna) within our community and beyond. At JSOWR, we organize religious and cultural events. These activities include celebrations of significant Jain festivals like Paryushan Parva, Mahavir Janma Kalyanak, Sangh Parna, and Samvatsari. Our society also places great importance on fostering community bonds through social gatherings such as picnics and family events. These occasions offer members the opportunity to connect, share experiences, and strengthen relationships within the Jain community. JSOWR is dedicated to teaching and preserving the principles of Jainism, ensuring that future generations understand and practice the values of our faith. By promoting education, community service, and cultural preservation, we strive to create a supportive and nurturing environment for all members. Through our collective efforts, the Jain Society of the Waterloo Region aims to make a positive impact on both our members and the wider community, guided by the timeless teachings of Jainism.
